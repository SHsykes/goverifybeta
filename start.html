Welcome to The Fictional City Council Go Verify portal. {% if serviceName %} {{ serviceName }} {% endif %}

This area allows you to upload documents so that you can apply for council services quickly. Your data is held under GDPR legislation.

Create a Go Verify account and save time:

  • report and apply for things at any time without needing to call us
  • save time with a simple and clear upload page
  • save a form and finish it later

Within this portal you can

  • provide your personal details (name, email address etc)
  • upload your proof of eligibility documents

The types of documents you can upload include

  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • Birth Certificates
  • Marriage and Civil Partnership certificates
  • Benefits eligibility letters
  • Utility bills
  • Council tax bill

Giving your consent

The portal will ask you to give your consent to the following actions:

  • the storage of your documents on secure council web servers.
  • access to your documents by authorised members of council staff

You can still apply for council services via this portal if you choose not to provide your documents.

If you can't upload documents

It's quicker to upload your documents online. If you're unable to do this, you have several options:

  • Visit your local council office. Our trained and vetted staff will help you to upload your documents. Find out more about supported upload service.
  • You can email your documents to admin@fictionalcitycouncil.gov.uk. If you emailed your documents, you will receive an automatic acknowledgement email. If you did not receive an email, check your junk folder.
