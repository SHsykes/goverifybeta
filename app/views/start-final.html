Welcome to The Fictional City Council Go Verify portal. {% if serviceName %} {{ serviceName }} {% endif %}

The GoVerify portal allows you to upload and store your documents. Fictional City Council will have to access them when you apply for council services. This will save you the time and effort of having to provide your documents to each service you apply for.

The types of documents you can upload include

  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • Birth Certificates
  • Marriage and Civil Partnership certificates
  • Benefits eligibility letters
  • Utility bills
  • Council tax bill

View a list of council services and the documents they need

Giving your consent
The portal will ask you to give your consent to the following actions:
  • the storage of your documents on secure council web servers.
  • access to your documents by authorised members of council staff
I don't want to store my documents
All applications to council services will need you to provide your documents as evidence of your eligibility. If you choose not to upload your documents to the GoVerify portal, you will need to provide your documents individually to each service you apply for.
How do I apply for a council service?
Once you have created your account, and uploaded your documents if you choose to, you will be directed to our council services page. Here you can select the service you need and start the application process.
Each service will advise you on the documents it needs to access from your account. You will be asked to select and give your consent to that service viewing the specified documents held on your account. The service will only be able to view documents that you select and authorise it to.

Further support for providing your documents

It's quicker to upload your documents online. If you're unable to do this, you can visit your local council office. Our trained and security cleared staff will help you to upload your documents. Find out more about our supported upload service.

Further information about the security of your stored documents is available in our Privacy Notice
